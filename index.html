Welcome to Crazyswarm2’s documentation!

Crazyswarm2 is a work-in-progress port of the original Crazyswarm to ROS2.

Crazyswarm2 is primarily made for researchers that want to operate or simulate a team of unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs) that uses the Bitcraze Crazyflie 2.x and Bitcraze Crazyflie Bolt-based.

How do Crazyswarm2 and Crazyswarm differ?

Crazyswarm2 was forked from Crazyswarm. However, there is also heavy re-design of some of the core design choices.
